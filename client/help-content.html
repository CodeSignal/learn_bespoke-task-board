Task Board Help

Columns

The board has four columns representing task statuses: Pending, In Progress, Blocked, and Done.

Adding Tasks

Click the + button in any column header to add a new task to that column. Enter a title and optionally a description, then click Add.

Moving Tasks

Drag and drop cards between columns to change their status. Cards can be reordered within a column as well.

Persistence

All tasks are saved in your browser's local storage and will persist across page refreshes.
